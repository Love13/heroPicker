Dota 2 Hero Picker

Advantage measures the matchup between two heroes regardless of thir normal win rate. It is calculated by establishing their win rates both in and outside of the matchup and comparing the difference against a base win rate. The calculation is procedural and advantage/disadvantage results are not designed to be symmetrical.
